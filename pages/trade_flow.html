Session Window: Silver Bullet 10 AM - 11 AM (NY Time)

  • High-probability and High-push moves occur after a break of the 200 EMA, Trendline, or Break & Retest.
  • Major impulsive moves align with the entry trend, without immediate high-volume engulfing candles in the opposite direction.
  • Expect a retest at key zones such as the 0.382 Fibonacci level, Fair Value Gaps (FVGs), or Order Blocks (OBs).
  • Typically forming below the 100 EMA
  • Candlestick are clean and readable, and not confusing.
  • Price often returns to the 100 EMA or 200 EMA for a retest, triggering liquidation of early entries.
  • Creates proper FVGs and occasional Inducements which frequently align with Order Blocks formed after liquidity sweeps.
  • A+ entries occur near the 200 EMA where OBs and FVGs coincide with liquidity events.
  • Fibonacci retracements often break the 0.5 level but avoid breaching 0.8 is worth noting.
  • Wicks can provide ideal entry opportunities.
  • Entry candles may leave a clear confirmation signal.
